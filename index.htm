Welcome to FYMS, the ultimate platform for student project management! FYMS, revolutionizes the way students, supervisors, and coordinators interact. With FYMS, students can seamlessly upload their tasks, whether it's a research paper, project proposal, or presentation. Coordinators have full control to manage students and supervisors, facilitating smooth communication and coordination.
